ABOUT US

Aakaar is the annual festival of the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Bombay comprising a range of engaging competitions, workshops, lecture series, panel discussions, industrial talks, exhibitions, and various entertaining learning events. Since its inception in 2009, it has become Asia’s largest Departmental College festival. Aakaar serves as a forum for discussion and offers opportunities for developing new skills for engineering students across India.

ABOUT AAKAAR

The College Representative Program is the core of Aakaar’s outreach initiative. We at Aakaar hope to connect with college students across India to pique their interest in Civil Engineering and provide a platform to pursue the same. The College Representative Program aids in the realization of this goal. The College Representative Program enables you to create a strong network of like-minded people who can support your future professional pursuits while gaining invaluable experience, organizing unique events, and putting on spectacular events.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does it mean to be a College Representative?

As a College Representative, you will be the face of Aakaar in your institute. You will be the first point of contact for any student who requires information about Aakaar, and you will be assisting them as and when needed.

What are the eligibility requirements for joining the program?

Students from any college, stream, year of college can register for the College Representative program

How can I register for the College Representative program?

For registration, you need to click on Google Sign In in Register Now section. Once registration is complete, you will not be able to change your email address.

How much time and effort do I need to invest?

On average, you are expected to put in 1-2 hours per week. It is crucial to maintain devotion and honesty toward the tasks given to you.

How will I receive updates about tasks?

You will be informed about tasks through CR dashboard and social media handles as well as Whatsapp/Telegram groups.
